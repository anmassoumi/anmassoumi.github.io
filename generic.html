About Me

Hello there! My name is Alex Massoumi, and I am a passionate and driven student at the University of California, Irvine pursuing my passion for Aerospace Engineering.

Ever since I was a child, I have been captivated by the wonders of space flight and the intricacies of vehicle machinery. My fascination with aerospace and mechanical engineering stems from a deep-rooted curiosity about how things work.

Currently I am actively seeking an internship in either aerospace or mechanical engineering. I firmly believe that hands-on experience complements theoretical knowledge, and I am enthusiastic about applying my skills while learning from industry professionals.

My ultimate aspiration is to contribute to groundbreaking advancements in space exploration and technology, or be a part of the next Constructors Championship winning F1 team. Whether it is designing next-generation spacecraft, innovating propulsion systems, or fine-tuning an F1 engine, my motivation stems from pushing the boundaries of what is achievable in aerospace and mechanical engineering.

In summary, I am a dedicated and ambitious student with a fervent interest in aerospace and mechanical engineering, a love for outdoor activities, and a steadfast determination to leave a lasting impact on the field. I am eagerly anticipating an exciting journey ahead as I strive to turn my dreams into reality.
